How it works?


4 Steps to get the result


Step 1 : Select Category
This step provides you an access to the detail of 7 categories which are: Natural Resources, Resource Efficiency, Resource Use In Major Sectors, Consumption-based Indicators for Natural Resource Use, Trade Dependency, Resources and Human Development, and Inclusive Green Recovery. Let's start by selecting the most global trend such as 'Natural Resource', and see the result.


Step 2 : Select Country
This step provides you an access to information gathered from the selected country. Let's try expanding the country list, and get the result of your country.


Step 3 : Select Indicator
This step will bring you deeper in to the pool of information about each indicators under your selected category. Meanwhile, the Material Use under Natural Resource will always a default ourcome. Let's select one which you are interested to explore more, and see the amzing result!


Step 4 : Select Visualization
Once you complete Step 1 - 3, this step will be execute automatically. Let's expand the result, you will get all information you need!



Export Data
To use this feature, you must choose which format you need to export. This visualization provides 3 different data format which are: Excel, CSV, and JPG. Let's get the best one which will be saved in your device.


Share Chart
Sharing the chart to friend, colleges is just one click. Click the "Share" button and save the link!

Use of Search Box


Congratulation! you are now expert on our platform.
Let's try typing your query in the search box, once it is done, navigate all the relevant visualiation and information you are looking for.

Through this platform, you will always be able to access more inforamtion of paticular indicator with its own visualization in a separate page. All the features are there.
